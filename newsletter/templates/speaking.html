The Freedom Fires

The Freedom Fires is a step by step process designed to assist conscious leaders to look within, so that they can burn through the fear and the doubts that inhibit our ability to step into the greatest level of service possible for our lives.

Speaking Topics

FREEDOM FIRES - Leading Through The Fires Of Change

In this presentation Annastacia will take the audience through a step by step process that will enable leaders to tap into the infinite power of Freedom Fires. This is a process enabling leaders to navigate through a crisis and to come out the other side feeling more empowered, on purpose and able to reach for a much higher potential as a human being.

THE POSSIBILITY PLAINS - Under The Skies Of Limitless Wonder

In this presentation, Annastacia dares the audience to dream in a way they may have never considered possible before. This is a process of empowerment designed to inspire the pioneers in an organisation with the tools to create something new, or to innovate in a new way. This process bridges the gap between fear and possibility.

MAKE IT HAPPEN - A New Story Of Belonging

In this presentation, Annastacia takes the audience on a deeply personal journey back to the heartland of her native home in Kenya. In 2017 she returned to Kenya on a mission to adopt a child. In going on that journey she was exposed to the corruption of the Kenyan child welfare system and was exposed to the exploitation of both women and children. What was so shocking was the level of cultural acceptance around abuse of children and women. As a result, Annastacia founded Make It Happen, an organisation on a mission to advocate for the rights of children who are being abused, exploited or put at risk within family homes or within the public system.
