Abundance, where can I find it?

August 6, 2021

This is the month of abundance.

In Feng Shui, this is the month of moving spirits also known as the "Ghost Month". It came from the Chinese belief that during the Seventh Lunar month, Ksitigarbha in charge of the underworld would open the gates of hell on the first day to let out the hungry ghosts and close it on the 30th day of the same month.

This is a time to not look into drastic changes and sit down and appreciate what you have. During the seventh Lunar month, You are not allowed to move houses, for example, and settle in the abundance of what is.

I wonder what is it you need not shift in your life this month so you can sit on it and learn to live in abundance? To achieve this, we need to state our intentions so we can achieve clarity.

You may be asking, but Annastacia with all that's is going on around me how can I be in abundance?

I hear you.

What is Abundance?

Abundance means having more than what you need. Since the very beginning, we've been taught that the world is complex and you'll need this and that to survive. But what we fail to always come back to is the beginning of it all - yourself.

You took yourself where you're at now, you taught yourself what you need to know, you have achieved a lot because of you! You are the star of this show. Yes, you! You have been created for this very moment.

Everything you have ever needed and you will ever need is inside you.

For you to experience the true abundance and be able to swim in the ocean of it, you have to know, believe, and act from the inside. Everything comes from within. Once you understand your true value and worth and can show yourself self-love, everything else will fall into place.

Practices for Self-Love

To show yourself self-love you have to give yourself the attention, care, and affirmation that you need.

Resting when you are tired

Creating boundaries so that no one ever disrespects you again

Spend 10 mins by yourself so you can silence the noise

Go for a run so you can jumpstart your active life

Eat a healthy meal to nourish your body

Listen to something uplifting so you can energise your spirit

Dance so you can elevate your energy

Avoid the news so you can protect your energy

Tell the people you love how much they mean to you so you can maintain healthy relationships

Pay attention to your spending so you can honour your finances

Enrol in coaching so you can keep yourself accountable and continue to grow

Whatever self-love is to you is, it's the first step to becoming self-aware. That means you can be present at the moment, be intentional about who you are and what you are with clarity. All of these equals abundance.

The Principle of Abundance

Remember it's all about perception. Energy grows where energy goes. Whatever you think more of in life you will attract it.

There is no scarcity in the abundance of everything - love, money, wealth, support, grace, sex, joy, health anything you want is there in abundance.

Your Journey to Abundance
